Source:

Method:
- Codex + Figma MCP metadata/design-context pull (partial; hit seat call limit during deeper extraction).

## 1) Product goals
- Reduce meal-planning stress for families/households.
- Capture household context (size, allergies, restrictions, appetite/capacity).
- Generate weekly meal plans with prep time, servings, and macro context.
- Convert plans into actionable shopping lists with estimated costs.
- Support weekly reflection/recap and progress loops.

## 2) Core user flows
1. Onboarding and intent setting
2. Member profiling and restrictions
3. Meal discovery/planning with filters
4. Meal detail drill-down (ingredients/macros)
5. Shopping list generation
6. Weekly reflection and recap

## 3) Feature inventory
- Progress-tracked onboarding wizard
- Household/member cards + profile attributes
- Restriction/preference capture tiles
- Weekly meal calendar and meal-type tabs
- Meal cards with prep/servings/macros/freshness
- Filter chips (all meals / quick / family favorites)
- Ingredient + pricing details
- Shopping list bundles and estimated totals
- Reflection timeline cards + testimonial/feedback style blocks

## 4) Data entities implied
- Household
- Member
- Preference/Restriction
- Meal/Recipe
- MealPlanSlot
- Ingredient/ShoppingListItem
- ReflectionEntry
- KPI/Stats

## 5) UI patterns/components
- Rounded card/tile system
- Segment controls + chip filters
- Macro summary blocks/tables
- Shopping rows with quantity/price/status chips
- Calm, high-whitespace mobile-first wireframe patterns

## 6) Risks / unknowns
- Extraction depth constrained by student-seat MCP call limits.
- Some visuals and interaction details are inferred, not fully confirmed.
- Validation/error/offline/sync behaviors not explicit in wireframes.
